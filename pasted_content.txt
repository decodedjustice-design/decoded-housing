## **Part 1: The Discovery Engine (Search & Maps)**

### **The Critique**
Current list-based views create "choice paralysis." Users in affordable housing markets need geographic context (proximity to transit) and instant eligibility verification.

### **The Upgrades**
* **Map-First Layout:** A split-pane view (Map on top/left, List on bottom/right) where pins refresh as the user pans.
* **The Eligibility Shield:** A persistent header where users enter "Household Size" and "Annual Income." The map then "greys out" or hides properties where the user is over the income limit.
* **Transit Overlays:** Integrated badges on property cards showing distance to the nearest [Sound Transit](https://www.soundtransit.org/) or Metro line.

---

## **Part 2: The Conversion Engine (Scripts & Calling)**

### **The Critique**
Static scripts cause "context-switching friction." Users lose their place when moving between the browser and the phone dialer.

### **The Upgrades**
* **Teleprompter Mode:** A "Call with Script" button that triggers the dialer and overlays a sticky "Bottom Sheet" with the script so the user can read it while on speakerphone.
* **Variable Injection:** Auto-populating the user's name and the property's specific program (ARCH/MFTE) directly into the text of the [Phone Scripts](https://decodedhous-3nnbt3jb.manus.space/scripts).
* **The Status Logger:** A post-call prompt asking: *"Did they answer? Is the waitlist open?"* This data feeds back into the search results in real-time.

---

## **Part 3: Visual & Trust Signals**

### **The Critique**
Lack of photos leads to a "Trust Deficit." Users often assume affordable units are lower quality than market-rate units.

### **The Upgrades**
* **The Zillow Gallery:** Replacing thumbnails with a 3-photo mosaic (Exterior, Interior, Amenity) for every listing.
* **Verification Timestamps:** Adding "Community Verified" badges (e.g., *"Verified by a user 2 hours ago"*) to property cards to prove the data isn't stale.
* **Resource Action Dashboard:** Transforming the [Resources Page](https://decodedhous-3nnbt3jb.manus.space/how-it-works) into a grid of "Action Cards" with clear icons for Financial Help, Legal Aid, and Paperwork Checklists.
